Welcome to The official campaign page for Christina Calugcugan “CC”, Candidate for SEIU Local 1000 President.

About Christina Calugcugan

“CC” has been a CA State Worker with the Employment Development Department for over 15 years and is currently serving as the Vice President of DLC 724, a delegate of the Los Angeles Labor Fed and is also a Regional Vice President for the Asian Pacific Islander Caucus with the SEIU International Union and brings years of experience and dedication to the labor movement.

With a passion for advocating for workers' rights and improving the lives of SEIU members, CC has organized and helped unionize health care workers in San Diego, the State Bar workers at Local 1000 and also helped Virginia Government workers unionize.

CC is strong woman of faith and is an active volunteer at her church and in her community and is committed to leading Local 1000 with integrity, transparency, and inclusivity.

CC’s Message

“It is time for State workers to come together and demand the respect and pay that we deserve! We are public servants and serve ALL Californians but we are getting paid lower than the people we provide services too and work under conditions that put our health and safety at risk. We must stand up for what is RIGHT and FIGHT for a living wage where our state workers are not needing to work two full-time jobs to make ends meet and where we are not living in our cars because we cannot afford rent. We must come together and strengthen our union that will secure our pensions, our benefits and a living wage where we can thrive and as your Local 1000 President, I will make sure this work gets done!”

Vision for SEIU

My Vision for SEIU Local 1000 is to strengthen our membership and identify and develop quality leaders that will help sustain our local for the years to come. To implement structures and programs that will develop our member leaders and educate all our members on our contract. Where we have the training and education needed to enforce and exercise our rights, where we as CA State Workers have livable wages and the power where the State takes care of the workers that care and provide services to all Californians.

We will achieve this by

  • Strengthening union solidarity and member engagement
  • Fighting for livable wages, benefits, and working conditions for all SEIU members
  • Advance diversity, equity, and inclusion within the union and in the workplace
  • Champion legislative and policy initiatives that support workers' rights
  • Promote union growth and empower SEIU locals to thrive
